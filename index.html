About me

Hey there, internet wanderers! 👋 I'm Fouzia Iffat, your friendly neighborhood web developer with a twist. Hailing from the vibrant lands of Odisha, I took a scenic route to the world of coding. Picture this: a commerce grad (yep, that's BCom in my back pocket!), who dove headfirst into the mesmerizing sea of IT. 🎓➡️💻

No classrooms or stern professors here – my journey into coding was self-charted, powered by sheer curiosity and an insatiable thirst for all things tech. It's like assembling a thousand-piece puzzle, but the picture is the internet, and every line of code I write is a piece that fits just right. 🧩

When I'm not busy translating coffee into code, you can find me geeking out over the latest tech trends or daydreaming about the next big web innovation. I believe that technology is a canvas, and I'm here with my palette of codes to paint a world where creativity meets functionality. 🎨💻

So, whether you're here to explore my digital creations or just passing by, welcome to my corner of the web – where commerce meets coding, and every day is a new adventure in the land of ones and zeroes! 🌐🚀
